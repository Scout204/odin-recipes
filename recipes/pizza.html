The Best Pizza Recipe

Description

Indulge in the bold flavors of a Detroit-styled pizza, a culinary masterpiece that pays homage to Motor City's culinary heritage. Characterized by its rectangular shape, Detroit pizza boasts a uniquely thick and airy crust, crowned with a crispy, caramelized edge that forms from the cheese reaching the pan's edges. Generously layered with a rich tomato sauce on top of the cheese, this pizza harmoniously balances the interplay between the savory toppings, creating a satisfying contrast of textures and flavors. With its innovative presentation and robust taste, Detroit-styled pizza offers a slice of tradition with a modern twist.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Toppings

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded brick or Wisconsin cheddar cheese (traditionally used for Detroit-style)
  • Pepperoni slices or other desired toppings

Sauce

  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

Dough Preperation

  1. In a small bowl, combine lukewarm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine bread flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise for about 1-2 hours, until doubled in size.

Sauce Preperation

  1. In a bowl, mix crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper. Adjust seasonings to taste.

Pizza Assembly

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (232°C).
  2. Coat a Detroit-style pizza pan or rectangular baking pan with 2-3 tablespoons of oil, making sure to cover the bottom and sides.
  3. Gently stretch and press the risen dough into the pan, covering the bottom and slightly up the sides.
  4. Layer shredded brick or cheddar cheese on top of the dough, ensuring it reaches the edges to create a crispy edge.
  5. Add your desired toppings, such as pepperoni slices or other ingredients.

Baking

  1. Spoon the sauce evenly over the toppings, making sure to cover the pizza.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and the crust is golden brown.
Slice up your Detroit-styled pizza into squares and enjoy the delicious blend of flavors and textures that make this unique style so special.
