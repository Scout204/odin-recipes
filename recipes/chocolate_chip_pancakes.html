The Best Chocolate Chip Pancake Recipe

Decription

Indulge in the ultimate breakfast delight with chocolate chip pancakes. These fluffy stacks of goodness blend the comfort of classic pancakes with the irresistible allure of rich chocolate chips. Each bite is a symphony of textures and flavorsâ€”soft and airy pancakes speckled with melty pockets of chocolate. Whether drizzled with maple syrup or enjoyed as they are, chocolate chip pancakes offer a decadent morning experience that's both nostalgic and utterly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk (or buttermilk for extra fluffiness)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips (semi-sweet or milk chocolate)

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients and gently mix until just combined. It's okay if there are a few lumps.
  4. Gently fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Preheat a griddle or a non-stick skillet over medium heat. If needed, lightly grease the surface with butter or oil.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of the pancake batter onto the griddle for each pancake. If desired, add a few extra chocolate chips on top of each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges start to look set, usually about 2-3 minutes.
  8. Carefully flip the pancakes using a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, or until the pancakes are golden brown and cooked through.
  9. Remove the pancakes from the griddle and repeat the process with the remaining batter.
  10. Serve the chocolate chip pancakes warm, topped with butter, additional chocolate chips, and maple syrup.
Enjoy your delicious chocolate chip pancakes as a delightful breakfast treat!
