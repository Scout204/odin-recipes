The Best Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

Savor the timeless delight of a cheeseburger a culinary masterpiece that brings together the savory succulence of a seasoned beef patty, the creamy melt of cheese, and the fresh crunch of lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and pickles. Nestled within a toasted bun, the harmonious marriage of flavors and textures offers a comforting, handheld indulgence that has earned its place as an iconic symbol of American comfort food.

Ingredients

For the burger patties:

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat for best flavor)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings:

  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Pickles

Condiments

  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare to medium, or until your preferred level of doneness is reached.
  3. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly golden.
  4. Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the bottom bun.
  5. Place a lettuce leaf on top of the sauce-covered bun
  6. Place the cooked patty on top of the lettuce.
  7. Add sliced tomatoes, sliced onions, and pickles on top of the patty.
  8. If you prefer melted cheese, place a slice of cheese on each patty immediately after removing them from the heat. Cover the patties with a lid or aluminum foil to help the cheese melt from the residual heat.
Your cheeseburger is now ready to be enjoyed! You can serve it with a side of french fries, potato chips, or coleslaw for a classic meal.
